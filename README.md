## Project Overview

The **Swiggy Sales & Operations Dashboard** provides an end-to-end view of Swiggy’s performance across multiple business dimensions.  
It uses a clean, Swiggy-themed color palette and structured layout to display insights clearly and professionally.

This dashboard helps analyze:
- Monthly Sales Trends  
- Daily & Weekly Order Behavior  
- Veg vs Non-Veg Distribution  
- Top Cities by Revenue  
- State-wise Sales Map  
- Ratings & Review Performance  
- Restaurant Contribution  
- Quarterly Summary  

It serves as a practical example of converting raw Excel data into a **business-ready analytical dashboard**.

## Insights Generated

### 1. Monthly Trends  
- Sales show clear growth over time.  
- Highest peaks observed in **May and June**.  
- Early months maintain stable but moderate performance.

### 2. Daily Trends  
- **Saturday** is the strongest day for revenue.  
- **Monday** shows the lowest sales activity.  
- Fridays and Sundays also generate higher traffic.

### 3. Food Category Contribution  
- **Non-Veg contributes 65%** of overall revenue.  
- Veg accounts for the remaining 35%.  
- Indicates higher customer preference for non-veg dishes.

### 4. Regional Sales Distribution  
Top states by revenue:
- Karnataka  
- Maharashtra  
- Telangana  

The map chart gives a clear geographic representation of demand.

### 5. City-wise Revenue Ranking  
1. **Bengaluru** (highest sales)  
2. Lucknow  
3. Hyderabad  
4. Chennai  
5. New Delhi  

These cities represent Swiggy’s strongest markets.
